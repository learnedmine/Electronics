FAQs

Common Questions

Still have questions? We're happy to help via WhatsApp anytime.

💬 Ask on WhatsApp
How long does a typical repair take?+

Most phone repairs (screens, batteries, ports) are completed within 1–3 hours. Laptop repairs may take 1–2 days depending on parts availability. We'll give you an accurate estimate after diagnostics.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?+

Yes! All repairs come with a warranty. Screen replacements carry a 3-month warranty, and other hardware repairs carry a 1-month warranty on parts and labour.

Can you come to my home or office?+

Yes, for some repairs we offer home/office pickup and delivery within Nairobi. Contact us via WhatsApp to check availability in your area and confirm pickup fees.

Is my data safe during repairs?+

Absolutely. We handle your device with full confidentiality. We recommend backing up your data before bringing in your device, but we never access your personal files during repairs.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept M-Pesa, cash, and bank transfers. Payment is collected upon completion of the repair. No deposit required for standard repairs.

Do you use genuine parts?+

We use high-quality OEM and compatible parts sourced from trusted suppliers. We'll always inform you about the part type and you can choose based on your budget.
